# Phase 5: Beat-em-ups & Brawlers (Lessons 2049-2432)

**Total:** 384 lessons across 8 tiers (48 lessons per tier)

**Phase Goal:** Master depth-axis movement and group combat. Build complete beat-em-ups with crowd management and co-op mechanics.

**Prerequisites:** Phase 2 (Double Dragon foundation), Phase 4 (advanced combat systems)

**Phase Philosophy:**
- Depth = pseudo-Y axis for positioning
- Group AI = surround, flank, attack timing
- Co-op = 2-4 players simultaneously
- 48 lessons sufficient for focused genre
- Complete games with multiple stages

**Game Progression:**
- **Tier 1:** Final Fight - 3-button combat, special moves, scrolling
- **Tier 2:** Streets of Rage - Desperation attacks, weapon durability
- **Tier 3:** Golden Axe - Magic system, rideable mounts
- **Tier 4:** TMNT Arcade - 4-player co-op, character variety
- **Tier 5:** Bad Dudes - Wall climbing, vehicle stages
- **Tier 6:** Renegade - Gang fights, motorcycle combat
- **Tier 7:** Target Renegade - Improved combat, boss variety
- **Tier 8:** Kung-Fu Master - Floor-by-floor, simplicity

**What Students Master:**
- Depth sorting (Y-axis draw order)
- Group AI (surround, flank, attack timing)
- Grab/throw systems
- Combo systems
- Weapon pickups (durability, variety)
- Co-op mechanics (4-player)
- Scrolling with depth
- Crowd management (spawning, despawning)
- Environmental weapons
- Beat-em-up pacing

## Tier 1: Final Fight (Lessons 2049-2096)

**Goal:** Build Final Fight with 3-button combat, special moves, and side-scrolling.

**Why this game:** Definitive arcade beat-em-up. Sets the standard for depth combat and pacing.

**Game Requirements:**
- 3 characters (Haggar, Cody, Guy) - different stats/moves
- 3-button combat: Attack, Jump, Special
- Combo system (punch-punch-kick, etc.)
- Grab and throw
- Special moves (costs health)
- 6 stages with boss
- Food items restore health
- Weapon pickups (knife, pipe, katana)
- 2-player co-op
- Lives and continues

**Skills Introduced:**
- Depth-axis movement (walk toward/away from camera)
- Depth sorting (characters draw based on Y position)
- 3-button combat system
- Combo strings (timed button sequences)
- Grab system (hold enemy, throw or attack)
- Special move (area attack, costs health)
- Environmental weapons
- Food pickups (health restoration)

### Lesson Structure

**Lessons 2049-2054: Depth Movement**
- L2049-2050: Y-axis movement (toward/away from camera)
- L2051-2052: Depth boundaries (walkable area)
- L2053-2054: Depth sorting algorithm (Y position = draw order)

**Lessons 2055-2060: Combat System**
- L2055-2056: 3-button inputs (attack, jump, special)
- L2057-2058: Punch combo (3-hit string)
- L2059-2060: Kick attacks

**Lessons 2061-2066: Grab System**
- L2061-2062: Grab enemy (close range + button)
- L2063-2064: Throw enemy (direction + attack)
- L2065-2066: Attack while holding (knee, headbutt)

**Lessons 2067-2072: Special Moves**
- L2067-2068: Special attack (area damage)

- L2071-2072: Invincibility frames

**Lessons 2073-2078: Enemies**
- L2073-2074: Basic thug (grab and punch)
- L2075-2076: Knife thug (weapon enemy)
- L2077-2078: Fat enemy (tank, more health)

**Lessons 2079-2084: Group AI**
- L2079-2080: Surround player (positioning)
- L2081-2082: Attack timing (not all at once)
- L2083-2084: Flank from behind

**Lessons 2085-2090: Items & Weapons**
- L2085-2086: Food drops (health restore)
- L2087-2088: Weapon pickups (knife, pipe, katana)
- L2089-2090: Weapon durability (break after X hits)

**Lessons 2091-2096: Stages & Polish**
- L2091-2092: 6 stages (scrolling)
- L2093-2094: Boss battles
- L2095-2096: 2-player co-op, lives, continues

## Tier 2: Streets of Rage (Lessons 2097-2144)

**Goal:** Build Streets of Rage with desperation attacks and refined combat.

**Why this game:** Improves on Final Fight formula. Desperation mechanic adds strategy.

**Game Requirements:**
- 3 characters (Axel, Blaze, Adam)
- Desperation attack (special without health cost, but limited uses)
- Running attacks (dash + attack)
- Back attack (attack behind without turning)
- Weapon variety (bat, pipe, bottle)
- Police support (call for artillery strike)
- 8 stages
- Grand upper (signature moves per character)

**Skills Introduced:**
- Desperation attack (limited uses, no health cost)
- Running mechanics (build speed, running attacks)
- Back attack (situational awareness)
- Support call (screen-clear, limited)
- Character-specific signature moves

## Tier 3: Golden Axe (Lessons 2145-2192)

**Goal:** Build Golden Axe with magic system and rideable mounts.

**Why this game:** Fantasy setting. Magic adds range. Mounts change gameplay.

**Game Requirements:**
- 3 characters (barbarian, dwarf, amazon) - different magic
- Magic system (collect potions, unleash magic)
- Rideable mounts (dragon, chicken-leg) - temporary power
- Potion thieves (steal your potions at night)
- Fantasy enemies (skeletons, knights, demons)
- Boss battles (Death Adder)
- 2-player co-op

**Skills Introduced:**
- Magic system (collect resource, cast spell)
- Mount system (ride creature, different attacks)
- Potion thieves (enemy that runs away, steals resource)
- Fantasy setting adaptation

## Tier 4: TMNT Arcade (Lessons 2193-2240)

**Goal:** Build TMNT with 4-player simultaneous co-op.

**Why this game:** 4-player co-op is complex. Character variety. Licenses mechanics.

**Game Requirements:**
- 4 turtles (different weapons, same stats)
- 4-player simultaneous co-op
- Weapon variety (katana, bo staff, sai, nunchaku)
- Pizza pickups (health)
- Vehicle stages (Turtle Van, surfing)
- Boss fights (Bebop, Rocksteady, Shredder)
- Lives shared across players

**Skills Introduced:**
- 4-player simultaneous (collision, coordination)
- Character differentiation (weapons, not stats)
- Vehicle stages (change gameplay mode)
- Shared lives pool (team resource)

## Tier 5: Bad Dudes (Lessons 2241-2288)

**Goal:** Build Bad Dudes with wall climbing and variety.

**Why this game:** Adds vertical movement. Vehicle stages. Ninja theme.

**Game Requirements:**
- 2 bad dudes (Striker, Blade)
- Wall climbing (vertical movement)
- Vehicle stages (motorcycle, helicopter)
- Ninja enemies (shuriken throwers, sword ninjas)
- Boss battles
- "President has been kidnapped by ninjas. Are you a bad enough dude to rescue the president?"

**Skills Introduced:**
- Wall climbing (vertical traversal in brawler)
- Vehicle combat stages
- Projectile enemies (shuriken)
- Stage variety (not all side-scrolling brawl)

## Tier 6: Renegade (Lessons 2289-2336)

**Goal:** Build Renegade with gang fights and motorcycle combat.

**Why this game:** Urban setting. Motorcycle stage is unique. Boss variety.

**Game Requirements:**
- Solo protagonist (Mr. K)
- 4 stages (subway, harbor, motorcycle chase, hideout)
- Boss at end of each stage (1v1 fight)
- Motorcycle stage (different gameplay)
- Kick, punch, jump kick
- Grab and knee
- Time limit per stage

**Skills Introduced:**
- 1v1 boss battles (before final boss)
- Motorcycle combat (different controls)
- Urban gang setting
- Time pressure per stage

## Tier 8: Kung-Fu Master (Lessons 2385-2432)

**Goal:** Build Kung-Fu Master with floor-by-floor progression.

**Why this game:** Classic simplicity. Vertical progression. Enemy variety. Synthesis of Phase 5.

**Game Requirements:**
- 5 floors, each with multiple stages
- Rescue Sylvia at top
- Enemy variety per floor (grippers, knife throwers, dragons, snakes, wasps)
- Time limit per floor
- Single life (continues allowed)
- Kick and punch
- Jump kick (flying kick)

**Skills Introduced:**
- Floor-by-floor progression (vertical structure)
- Enemy type variety (flying, ground, projectile)
- Simplicity as design (2 buttons, focused gameplay)
- Everything from Phase 5 synthesized
