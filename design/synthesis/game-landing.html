Shadowkeep

An isometric adventure in hand-written Z80 — built at the bar of Atic Atac, Knight Lore and Cybernoid II, the games that made the Spectrum feel bigger than its 48K. Arcs 1 & 2 ship now: thirty-two units that take you from an empty screen to a living, explorable keep — and real .sna binaries that run on the metal.

What it proves

01

Pixel-level sprite movement

Characters move by the pixel, not the 8×8 cell — smooth motion the hardware fights you on.

02

Y-sorted layered rendering

Back-to-front draw order so you can walk behind walls and pillars. The isometric depth trick.

03

Decoupled attribute & collision

Colour and collision handled apart from the bitmap — beating attribute clash on its own turf.
