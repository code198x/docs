- Save between levels

- Iconic music (Matt Gray)
- Cinematic presentation

**Skills Introduced:**
- Isometric perfection (best isometric engine on C64)
- Massive level count (11 detailed levels)
- NYC 1980s aesthetic (cultural zeitgeist)
