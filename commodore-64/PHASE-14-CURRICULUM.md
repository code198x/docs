## Tier 1: Boulder Dash (Lessons 7169-7232)

**Goal:** Build Boulder Dash with 150 levels and level editor.

**Why this game:** Perfect puzzle design. 150 hand-crafted levels. Difficulty curve mastery. Level editor. Commercial quality.

**Game Requirements:**
- 150 levels (A-E, 30 levels each cave)
- Grid-based puzzle (dig dirt, collect diamonds, avoid boulders)
- Physics (boulders fall when unsupported, crush you)
- Enemies (butterflies, fireflies explode into diamonds/boulders)
- Amoebas (grow, can be helpful or deadly)
- Magic walls (convert boulders to diamonds)
- Time limit per level
- Bonus for quick completion
- Extra lives
- **Level editor** (create and save custom levels)
- High score table

**Skills Introduced:**
- Massive content (150 levels!)
- Difficulty curve design (gradual increase, spikes, plateaus)
- Level editor creation (tools for content)
- Physics-based puzzles (emergent solutions)
- Commercial polish (every detail perfect)

- Time pressure (6-hour limit)
- Speech synthesis ("Stay a while... Stay FOREVER!")
