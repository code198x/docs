# Wanderer AGA: 32-Unit Outline

**Game:** 10 AGA Enhancement
**Platform:** Commodore Amiga (A1200/A4000)
**Language:** 68000 Assembly
**Units:** 32 (2 phases)
**Prerequisite:** Wanderer (OCS) complete
**Concept:** AGA-enhanced scrolling platformer with richer environments.

---

## Overview

Wanderer AGA brings the platformer to life with 256-colour environments. Richer parallax backgrounds, more detailed sprites, and atmospheric effects that weren't possible on OCS.

---

## AGA Enhancements

| Feature | OCS Version | AGA Version |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| World colours | 32 | 256 |
| Parallax layers | Limited palette | Full colour each |
| Player sprite | 16 colours | 64+ colours |
| Backgrounds | Dithered | Smooth gradients |
