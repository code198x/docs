# Ascension AGA: The Guru Meditation - 128-Unit Outline

**Game:** 16 AGA Enhancement
**Platform:** Commodore Amiga (A1200/A4000)
**Language:** 68000 Assembly
**Units:** 128 (8 phases)
**Prerequisite:** Ascension: The Guru Meditation (OCS) complete
**Concept:** The definitive AGA showcase - full 256-colour graphics, HAM8 cinematics, and A1200/A4000 optimisations.

## Overview

Ascension AGA is the ultimate expression of Amiga game development. Every region rendered in 256 colours, HAM8 cinematics throughout, enhanced effects, and optimisations that take advantage of the A1200's capabilities. This is the version that sits alongside Alien Breed 3D and Gloom as defining AGA titles.
