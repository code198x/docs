# Onslaught AGA: 32-Unit Outline

**Game:** 13 AGA Enhancement
**Platform:** Commodore Amiga (A1200/A4000)
**Language:** 68000 Assembly
**Units:** 32 (2 phases)
**Prerequisite:** Onslaught (OCS) complete
**Concept:** AGA-enhanced vertical shooter with richer bullet patterns and backgrounds.

---

## Overview

Onslaught AGA brings the bullet hell experience to full colour. More colourful bullet patterns, richer stage backgrounds, and enhanced explosion effects make the chaos even more visually impressive.

---

## AGA Enhancements

| Feature | OCS Version | AGA Version |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Bullet colours | Limited | Full spectrum |
| Backgrounds | 32 colours | 256 colours |
| Explosions | Basic palette | Rich gradients |
| Stage variety | Similar palettes | Distinct themes |
