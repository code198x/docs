# Fortress AGA: 32-Unit Outline

**Game:** 14 AGA Enhancement
**Platform:** Commodore Amiga (A1200/A4000)
**Language:** 68000 Assembly
**Units:** 32 (2 phases)
**Prerequisite:** Fortress (OCS) complete
**Concept:** AGA-enhanced isometric adventure with richer environments.

---

## Overview

Fortress AGA transforms the isometric world. Richer tile graphics, enhanced lighting effects, and more detailed character sprites bring the 3D illusion to life with 256 colours.

---

## AGA Enhancements

| Feature | OCS Version | AGA Version |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Tile colours | 32 | 256 |
| Lighting | Basic | Smooth gradients |
| Characters | Limited shading | Full shading |
| Zones | Similar palettes | Distinct themes |
