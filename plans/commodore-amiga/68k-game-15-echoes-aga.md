# Echoes of the Ancients AGA: 64-Unit Outline

**Game:** 15 AGA Enhancement
**Platform:** Commodore Amiga (A1200/A4000)
**Language:** 68000 Assembly
**Units:** 64 (4 phases)
**Prerequisite:** Echoes of the Ancients (OCS) complete
**Concept:** AGA-enhanced Metroidvania with 256-colour worlds and HAM8 cutscenes.

---

## Overview

Echoes AGA is the definitive version of the Metroidvania experience. Six zones each with distinct 256-colour palettes, atmospheric lighting throughout, and HAM8 cutscenes for key story moments. This is what Metroid would have looked like on AGA.

---

## AGA Enhancements

| Feature | OCS Version | AGA Version |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Zone colours | 32 per zone | 256 per zone |
| Atmosphere | Basic | Full lighting |
| Cutscenes | Static | HAM8 cinematics |
| Player sprite | 16 colours | 64+ colours |
| Effects | Limited | Rich particles |
