# Decision: British English

## The decision

All content uses British English spelling: colour, learnt, centre, behaviour.

**Exception**: "program" (not "programme") when referring to computer programs.

## Why

The project creator is British. The content should feel authentic to its voice. British English also connects naturally to the UK origins of several platforms (ZX Spectrum, BBC Micro).

## Enforcement

The `check-spelling` skill scans for American English spellings. Run before publishing.
